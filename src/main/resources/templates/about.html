Food & Friends is how People cook together!

we want to build a community where it is easy to to cook together!

For my semester project I have decided to write an exchange and communication platform website around the topic of food and recipes. Since I have already gained a foothold in the field of fullstack development professionally through a part-time job and internship, I would like to continue my education in this area in the future. For the semester project I wanted to educate myself personally and therefore I chose technologies with which I had no previous points of contact. This Platform is written in Kotlin + Spring Boot, which i had no previous knowledge of. You can checkout the source code on Github
